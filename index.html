Lexi's Tank

Meet the Fish!

Instead of labeling this Martin's Aquarium, I named this Lexi's Tank because my sister owns a tank and I thought it would be fun to include her fish. I've only named one, which is Po, the rest had been unnamed. I think I named another one, but I don't remember what the name was. Either way, I used all the fish types she had, avoiding mentioning how many of each she actually has as to not completely overwhelm myself. She has a lot of fish and I am not naming them all.
